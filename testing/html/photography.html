Hello! My name is Daniel Diaz

“What I like about photographs is that they capture a moment that’s gone forever, impossible to reproduce.” 
― Karl Lagerfeld

At the beginning of my college career I began taking pictures of anything and everything, devolping my skills daily. I became fascinated by the ability to make a memory tangable. Ever since then I have not been able to look back.

Before I knew it, I was the official University of California, Irvine Mock Trial Photographer. Specializing in event photography, I love to capture moments that can be remembered through an image.

Feel free to contact me for more information on how I can be your events next photographer.
